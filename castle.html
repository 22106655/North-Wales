Castles

North Wales is steeped in history and is populated by great castles from medieval strongholds to 19th century stately homes. Some castles were built as early as the 11th century but earlier fortifications and earthworks date back to the iron age.

Some of the most impressive castles in North Wales are...

  • Caernarfon
  • Chirk
  • Powis
  • Conwy
  • Harlech/li>
  • Criccieth
  • Beaumaris
  • Penrhyn
