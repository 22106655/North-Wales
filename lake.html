Lakes

North wales has many beautiful lakes and rivers, including Llyn Tegid, near Bala, which is the largest natural lake in Wales!

Other lakes include...

  • Llyn Ogwen, near Tryfan
  • Llyn Llydaw, near Snowdon
  • Llyn Padarn, near Llanberis
  • Llyn Peris, near Llanberis
  • Llyn Elsi, near Betws y Coed
  • Llyn Cowlyd
  • Llyn Celyn
  • Lake Vyrnwy
  • Marchlyn Mawr
